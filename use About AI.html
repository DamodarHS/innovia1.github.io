ABOUT ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence(AI) also known as machine intelligence, is the intelligence demonstrated by machines, in contrast to the natural intelligence displayed by humans and other animals, such as "LEARNING" and "PROBLEM SOLVING"

Is AI a new technology to the world?

AI is not a new word or a new technology for researchers. This technology is much older than you would imagine. Even there are myths of mechanical men in Ancient Greek and Egytian Myths.

What contributes to AI?

AI is a science and technology,based on disciplines such as Computer Science,Biology,Psycology,Mathematics,Linguistics,Engineering. A major thrust of AI is in the development of computer functions associated with human intelligence,such as reasoning,learning and problem solving. Out of the following areas,one or multiple areas can contribute to build an intelligent system.

Key research areas in AI

1.Problem Solving,Planing and Search-Generic problem solving architecture based on ideas from congnitive science(game playing,robotics). 2.Knowledge Reprensentation-To store and manipulate information(logical and probabilistic reprensentation). 3.Computer Vision-Processing visual information
